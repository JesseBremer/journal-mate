The Science Behind Roteform

Roteform's seven questions are carefully selected from decades of psychological research on well-being, personal growth, and mental health:

Research-Backed Questions

  • Gratitude Practice: Proven to increase life satisfaction by up to 25% (Seligman et al.)
  • Emotional Awareness: Core component of emotional intelligence and mental health
  • Values Alignment: Critical for intrinsic motivation and life satisfaction (Self-Determination Theory)
  • Growth Mindset: Builds resilience and promotes learning from challenges (Dweck)
  • Social Connection: Essential for longevity and psychological well-being (Harvard Study of Adult Development)
  • Self-Compassion: Reduces anxiety and increases psychological resilience (Kristin Neff)
  • Narrative Identity: Helps create coherent sense of self and purpose (Dan McAdams)

Why Journal?

Journaling has been proven to reduce stress, improve mental clarity, enhance creativity, and help process life experiences. Research shows that regular journaling can boost immune function, reduce symptoms of depression and anxiety, and improve overall life satisfaction. Journal-Mate makes it easier to build this beneficial habit by removing barriers and providing scientifically-optimized prompts for maximum psychological benefit.
